Biscayne World
2014 11min
Digital

An experimental animated documentary based on the
insane everyday interactions on Biscayne Boulevard,
as seen through the ubiquitous, iconic graffiti eyeballs
of street artist Ahol Sniffs Glue.

Created, Illustrated by: Alouishous San Gomma
