The Booth
2021/2022 7 min.
Spy Glasses, VHS, Digital, Archival


“The Booth” is an exploration of the history
and subculture surrounding the private viewing
booths hidden in the back of adult bookstores.

“The Booth” is about adult video arcades and the
role alternative spaces play in
shaping—and preserving—identities.
Through interviews, archival media, and site
visits, the documentary explores this refuge for
marginalized and at-risk communities and what
might happen if these spaces were to disappear.
